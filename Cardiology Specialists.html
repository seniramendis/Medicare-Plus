Cardiology Department

Advanced Heart Care from a Team You Can Trust

Our Cardiology Department is dedicated to the complete health of your heart. We provide a full spectrum of advanced cardiac care, from preventive screenings and risk assessment to the diagnosis and treatment of complex heart conditions. Our expert cardiologists and cardiac care teams specialize in managing chronic issues like hypertension (high blood pressure) and high cholesterol, as well as providing urgent care for acute events.

We offer state-of-the-art diagnostic services, including ECGs, Echocardiograms, and Stress Tests, to get a precise understanding of your heart's health. For patients recovering from a heart attack or cardiac surgery, our comprehensive rehabilitation program provides the medical support and lifestyle guidance necessary to regain strength and prevent future disease. Trust us to deliver expert, compassionate care for your most vital organ.

Services Include:

  • Interventional Cardiology (Angioplasty and Stenting)
  • Heart Failure & Disease Management
  • Arrhythmia (Irregular Heartbeat) Clinic
  • Post-Heart Attack Recovery & Rehabilitation
  • Preventive Screenings & Risk Assessment
  • Advanced Cardiac Diagnostic Lab
