Advanced Diagnostic Services

Precision and Technology in Medical Testing

The accuracy of your diagnosis is the foundation of effective treatment. Our Advanced Diagnostics center is equipped with state-of-the-art technology to provide physicians and patients with the clearest, most precise results. The center is divided into two core units: our high-tech Radiology & Imaging department and our comprehensive Clinical Laboratory.

Our imaging services provide detailed pictures of the body's internal structures, featuring advanced MRI, high-speed CT Scanners, Digital X-rays, 4D Ultrasound, and Mammography. Our fully-automated clinical laboratory runs a complete range of tests on blood, tissue, and other samples, from routine blood counts and cholesterol panels to specialized tests in microbiology, immunology, and histopathology.

Services Include:

  • Imaging: MRI, CT Scans, Digital X-Ray, 4D Ultrasound, Doppler Scans, Mammography
  • Laboratory: Clinical Pathology (Blood, Urine, Stool)
  • Histopathology (Biopsy and Tissue Analysis)
  • Microbiology (Culture and Infection Testing)
  • Immunology & Serology
  • Biochemistry (Organ Function, Cardiac Markers)
  • Hematology (Complete Blood Counts)
